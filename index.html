About

Think back to the countless times you have wished “Why couldn’t all of my studying and school assignment deadlines be in one place?” or “How can I study more effectively with my time without getting distracted by switching through apps”?


This is why we - as students - decided to create a Chrome extension to solve this problem.

Features

Upon opening up the extension, you will see many features including, a digital To-do List to manage and keep track of your homework. The Music Manager helps quickly control the music you want played while you study and will connect to your Spotify, Apple Music, or Google Music account. The ability to change whatever Wallpaper you want as a background to study & finally a Productivity Mode which allows you to block applications.
